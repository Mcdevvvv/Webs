ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology. It refers to the broad field encompassing technologies and tools used for managing and processing information, as well as facilitating communication and the exchange of data. ICT encompasses a wide range of technologies, including computers, software applications, telecommunications systems, networks, and the internet. ICT plays a significant role in various aspects of our lives, including education, business, government, healthcare, entertainment, and more. It enables the creation, storage, retrieval, and manipulation of digital information, making it easier to process and share data across different platforms and devices.
