HUMSS stands for Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand, which is an academic track or specialization in the Philippine education system. It is one of the four strands offered in the senior high school (SHS) program, which is a two-year period of education following the completion of junior high school.

Bachelor of Arts (BA) or Bachelor of Science (BS) in Psychology
Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Communication or Journalism
Bachelor of Arts (BA) in English or Literature
Bachelor of Arts (BA) in History
