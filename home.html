DepEd MISSION

To protect and promote and the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based and complete basic education, where; Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe and motivating environment Teachers facilitate and constantly nurture every learner and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen Famliy, community and other stakeholders are ectively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.
