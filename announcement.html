ANNOUNCEMENTS

BLGU SEMIRARA CONDUCTS FOGGING AT SEMIRARA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL With the aim to prevent diseases brought by mosquitoes, BLGU Semirara conducted fogging within SNHS Campus on March 24, 2023. Students were advised to temporarily vacate their classrooms to give way for the fogging activity and allowed to enter after fifteen minutes. Our sincerest thanks to BLGU Semirara for including the institution in your program.
